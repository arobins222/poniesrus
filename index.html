About Us

At Ponies "R" Us & Mobile Petting Zoo, we bring the ultimate farm experience right to you! Based in Downriver, Michigan, we specialize in creating unforgettable memories with our mobile pony rides, interactive petting zoos, and on-site farm adventures. Whether it’s a birthday party, school field trip, community event, or even a live nativity scene, our team delivers safe, fun, and educational experiences for guests of all ages. What We Offer Mobile Pony Rides: We bring our sweet, gentle ponies directly to your event. Children can ride, brush, and connect with the ponies in a safe and friendly environment. Mobile Petting Zoo: Meet and interact with a wide variety of cuddly animals — perfect for hands-on learning and fun. On-Site Farm Experiences: Visit our Downriver, Michigan farm for horseback trail rides, family-friendly events, and our popular Family Farm Hour. Specialty Events: From birthday parties to holiday celebrations and live nativity scenes, we make every occasion one to remember. Professional and Trusted Ponies "R" Us is licensed, insured, and proud to serve families, schools, and organizations throughout Michigan and Ohio. With years of experience and a true passion for animals, we’re dedicated to providing professional service, clean and well-cared-for animals, and an unforgettable farm experience wherever we go.
